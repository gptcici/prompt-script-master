# Seedance Real Lighting Rules

## Four-layer lighting formula

Use concrete, physical lighting instead of vague words such as `真实光影` or `电影感光影`.

Formula:

`main light source + layered light structure + character local detail + environment interaction`.

## Main light source

Specify direction, softness/hardness, and color temperature: 45-degree side light, side-back light, window slant light, overhead light, warm candle light, cold moonlight, neon side light.

## Layered structure

Describe key light, rim light, fill/bounce, and dark-side detail. Do not stack multiple equal main lights.

## Character detail

Attach light to visible anatomy/materials: nose bridge highlight, eye socket shadow with detail, cheekbone highlight, jawline shadow boundary, hair rim light, fabric fold light.

## Environment interaction

Use weak bounce from snow, water, wall, desk, stone floor, wet pavement, or clothing. Bounce light must not overpower the main light.

## Placement

For Seedance, place lighting after subject/action/expression/body linkage or after close-up face lighting, and before scene environment.
