# Real Camera Language Override

Use this file only as practical camera language for photoreal OpenAI image-family prompts. Treat all lens, shot size, angle, depth of field, and movement terms as real camera choices for AIMV / AI short-drama frames. Do not use camera terms as decorative cinematic jargon. The final prompt must still look photographed, not stylized or rendered.

# Cinematic Camera Language System

This reference turns a rough image idea into camera-ready visual language. Use it together with composition-space-structure.md.

## Core rule

Every prompt should include:

1. Lens choice.
2. Shot size.
3. Camera angle.
4. Depth of field.
5. Camera movement feeling when useful.
6. Focus hierarchy.

## Lens choice

### 24mm wide lens

Use for landscapes, architecture, sci-fi interiors, crowded streets, and strong spatial depth.

Chinese: 24mm 广角镜头，空间纵深明显，前景更有存在感，环境尺度被放大。

English: 24mm wide-angle lens, strong spatial depth, foreground presence emphasized, environment scale expanded.

### 35mm natural environmental lens

Use as the safest default for environmental portraits, street scenes, travel scenes, and realistic short-drama stills.

Chinese: 35mm 自然环境镜头，人物与环境关系自然，空间透视真实，适合中景和环境人像。

English: 35mm natural environmental lens, natural relationship between subject and environment, realistic perspective, suited for medium shots and environmental portraits.

### 50mm natural lens

Use for calm portraits, daily scenes, realistic product/lifestyle images, and balanced perspective.

Chinese: 50mm 标准镜头，接近自然观察感，主体比例稳定，背景不过度压缩。

English: 50mm normal lens, natural observational feeling, stable subject proportion, background not overly compressed.

### 85mm portrait lens

Use for portraits, fashion, emotional close-ups, soft background separation.

Chinese: 85mm 人像镜头，柔和背景压缩，人物面部与眼神成为主要焦点，浅景深。

English: 85mm portrait lens, gentle background compression, face and eyes as main focal point, shallow depth of field.

### 135mm long lens

Use for isolation, distance, surveillance feeling, compressed landscapes, loneliness, or emotional separation.

Chinese: 135mm 长焦镜头，压缩空间距离，背景贴近主体，制造孤独、疏离或被观察感。

English: 135mm long lens, compressed spatial distance, background pulled closer to subject, creating isolation or observed distance.

### Macro / detail lens

Use for product details, eyes, hands, texture, jewelry, food, flowers, mechanisms.

Chinese: 微距/细节镜头，焦点集中在材质纹理或关键细节，背景退成柔和色块。

English: macro/detail lens, focus concentrated on material texture or key detail, background fading into soft color blocks.

## Shot size

### Extreme wide shot

Use for scale, solitude, epic space, desert, ocean, sky, sci-fi structures.

Chinese: 大远景，主体在巨大环境中显得渺小，空间尺度和情绪压迫感清晰。

English: extreme wide shot, subject appears small within a vast environment, strong sense of scale and emotional pressure.

### Wide shot / full shot

Use for full-body action, posture, movement, landscape relation.

Chinese: 全景/远景，完整保留身体姿态和环境关系，动作线条清楚。

English: wide shot / full shot, full body and environment relationship preserved, action line readable.

### Medium shot

Use for character emotion plus environment.

Chinese: 中景，保留上半身动作和环境关系，情绪与空间同时可读。

English: medium shot, upper-body action and environment relationship preserved, emotion and space both readable.

### Close-up

Use for face, eyes, hands, object detail, emotional intensity.

Chinese: 近景/特写，焦点集中在眼睛、手部或关键物件，背景柔和退后。

English: close-up, focus concentrated on eyes, hands, or key object detail, background gently receding.

## Camera angle

### Eye level

Use for realism, intimacy, grounded emotion.

Chinese: 平视机位，真实、亲近、克制，观众像站在人物面前。

English: eye-level camera angle, realistic, intimate, restrained, as if the viewer stands before the subject.

### Low angle

Use for power, awe, heroism, architecture, threat.

Chinese: 低机位仰拍，主体更具力量感和压迫感，背景向上展开。

English: low-angle shot, subject feels powerful or imposing, background expanding upward.

### High angle

Use for fragility, isolation, vulnerability, smallness.

Chinese: 高机位俯拍，人物显得脆弱或渺小，空间关系更清楚。

English: high-angle shot, subject appears vulnerable or small, spatial relationship clearer.

### Over-the-shoulder

Use for dialogue, discovery, subjective viewpoint, tension.

Chinese: 过肩视角，前景肩部形成遮挡，观众被带入人物视线。

English: over-the-shoulder angle, foreground shoulder creates partial occlusion, viewer pulled into the character viewpoint.

### Ground-level angle

Use for running, dust, rain, footsteps, street texture, dramatic motion.

Chinese: 贴地低机位，前景地面纹理和动作冲击力更强。

English: ground-level low angle, foreground ground texture and motion impact intensified.

## Depth of field

### Shallow depth of field

Use for portraits, products, emotion, isolation.

Chinese: 浅景深，焦点锁定主体，前景和远景化为柔和色块，主体从环境中自然分离。

English: shallow depth of field, focus locked on the subject, foreground and background softened into color blocks, subject naturally isolated.

### Deep focus

Use for spatial storytelling, architecture, group scenes, sci-fi interiors, landscapes.

Chinese: 深焦，前景、中景、远景都有可读信息，但主体焦点最明确。

English: deep focus, foreground, middle ground, and background remain readable while the subject remains the clearest focal point.

### Rack focus feeling

Use only when describing a still that implies a real camera focus transition.

Chinese: 拉焦瞬间感，视觉注意力从前景物件转移到人物眼神。

English: rack-focus moment, visual attention shifting from a foreground object to the subject's eyes.

## Camera movement feeling

For still-image prompts, describe movement as an implied frame, not actual video, unless the user asks for video.

### Tracking feeling

Chinese: 跟拍感，镜头像与人物同步移动，背景有轻微方向性拖影。

English: tracking-shot feeling, camera moving with the subject, background carrying subtle directional motion softness.

### Dolly-in feeling

Chinese: 缓慢推近感，画面像在情绪爆发前一刻靠近人物。

English: slow dolly-in feeling, frame approaching the subject just before emotional intensity peaks.

### Handheld realism

Chinese: 轻微手持摄影感，画面真实、不完美、像纪录片式电影瞬间。

English: subtle handheld realism, imperfect documentary-like photographed moment.

### Static locked-off frame

Chinese: 固定机位，画面稳定克制，人物情绪在静止构图中慢慢显现。

English: locked-off static frame, stable and restrained, emotion unfolding inside still composition.

## Focus hierarchy

Use only one primary focal point.

- Portrait: eyes and micro-expression.
- Full-body person: face first, posture second.
- Product: label, silhouette highlight, or key material.
- Landscape: horizon, vanishing point, or light source.
- Architecture: perspective axis or vanishing point.
- Action: body motion, hands, or directional force.
- Animal: eyes and head silhouette.
- Sci-fi scale: relationship between tiny figure and vast structure.

## Mandatory camera phrase

Chinese: 镜头语言：[焦段]，[景别]，[机位]，[景深/运动感]；焦点层级：[主焦点]。

English: Camera language: [lens], [shot size], [camera angle], [depth of field / movement feeling]; focus hierarchy: [primary focal point].

## Quality check

Before final output, confirm:

- A lens is named.
- Shot size is named.
- Camera angle is named.
- Depth of field or movement feeling is named.
- Focus hierarchy has one primary focal point.
- Camera choices support the subject and emotion.
