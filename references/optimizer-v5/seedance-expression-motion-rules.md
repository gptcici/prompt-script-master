# Seedance Expression and Motion Rules

## Core rule

Do not describe emotion only as `happy`, `sad`, `angry`, `shy`, or similar labels. Every emotional beat must be visible through face and body.

## Required formula

`overall emotion baseline + eye action + brow action + mouth action + facial muscle state + transition rhythm + head/neck/shoulder/hand/body linkage`.

## Placement

Expression and body linkage must appear immediately after `subject + core action`, before lighting, scene, camera, and style.

## Rhythm

Use one core emotion and at most one transition direction per shot. Use words such as 缓缓, 逐渐, 平滑过渡, 自然延续, 小幅度变化.

## Singing/talking

Mouth movement must include jaw stability, lip opening range, breath rhythm, and face/body continuity.

## Stability wording

Keep expression and motion stability as positive, visible execution targets in the main prompt. Do not create a separate denial field.
